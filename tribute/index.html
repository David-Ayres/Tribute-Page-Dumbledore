Legacy

Even after his death, Dumbledore's influence and wisdom continued to guide Harry and his friends in their quest to defeat Voldemort. His legacy and teachings played a crucial role in shaping the outcome of the wizarding world's battle against evil. Albus Dumbledore's life was characterized by immense magical talent, leadership, and a complex personal history. He is remembered as one of the most influential and revered wizards in the Harry Potter series.
